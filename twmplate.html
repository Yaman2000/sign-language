WHAT IS SIGN LANGUAGE

Nov 12

Indian Sign language offers a huge collection of (ISL) signs. Each sign has an image, running video and threaded discussions. It is an ideal resource to use while you learn/teach Indian Sign Language. Each sign has an image, running video and threaded discussions. We are continually adding more signs and designing new services to empower the Deaf. Please share your ideas and comments and help us make this service better.
